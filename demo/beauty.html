FAQ

Common questions, answered.

How much does a website cost?

Packages range from $125 for a clean starter site up to $600 for a premium multi-page build. Most hairstylists, barbers, and small businesses land in the $125–$300 range depending on pages and features. Every project is custom, so scope drives final pricing — but you'll always see the full cost before work begins.

Do I need to pay everything upfront?

No. All projects are split into a deposit to begin and the remaining balance after you've reviewed and approved the finished site. For example, the Starter Website is $60 upfront and $65 at completion. You never pay everything at once.

How long does a project take?

Most projects complete within 1–3 weeks depending on the scope, available content, and feedback timing. Rush delivery is available for an additional $75 if you need it faster.

Do you build websites for hair stylists, barbers, and beauty brands?

Yes — this is one of the most common project types we work on. We understand what those businesses need: a polished look, a booking or contact form, mobile-first design, and a site that gets new clients to reach out. Check out the portfolio demos to see examples.

Can you redesign or update my existing website?

Absolutely. Redesigns are available as a project type when you fill out the quote form. Depending on what you need, we can update what's already there or rebuild it from scratch.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes. Monthly maintenance plans start at $25/month and cover updates, minor content changes, and general upkeep so your site stays current and running smoothly after launch.
